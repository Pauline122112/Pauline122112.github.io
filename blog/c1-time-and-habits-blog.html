Timebox and habits

The mindset of time management

03 December 2016

Which "Timebox and habits" techniques did you try?

The Timebox and habits techniques that I tried were Meditation, Habits and growth mindset, The Pomodoro technique and TimeBox.

What did you find interesting?

I found the Pomodoro Technique to be quite interesting. The idea that you will set the time to work for 25 minutes without interrupting myself seems like a technique that I would like to aquire. This technique will enable me to work with time and not against it. Eliminating the idea of becoming "burnt out". Managing distractions whether it's a call or social media updates.This technique will help me to log my distractions in order of priority. I hope to lead a balanced lifestyle and create a better work environment by using this technique.

What worked for you?

The Pomodore technique obviously works for me, however, I do hold high consideration for Meditation as I try to use this technique on a daily basis.

Which will you experiment further with?

I will be experiment further with the Pomodore Technique, I hope to save a lot of time using this technique.
