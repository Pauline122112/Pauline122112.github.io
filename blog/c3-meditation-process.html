    What did you think about meditation before reading the chapter?

    Before reading the chapter, I thought meditation was a form of relaxation, a way to calm the mind and therefore live a life full freedom and self expression.

    What new things hav you learn't about meditaton?

    • Meta attention is attention of attention, basically the ability to pay attention to attention itself.
    • Meditation gets you to a state where your mind is relaxed and alert at the same time.
    • When attention and meta-attention becomes strong, the mind becomes focused and stable.
    • When the mind is highly relaxed and alert, qualities such as calmness, clarity and happiness emerge.
    • Meditation is exercising the mind; training the mind so that it can gain more mental abilites.
    • After a few months of meditation, there is significant improvements with a persons health.
    • Creating good intentions is a form of meditation. Everytime an intention is created, it is subtly forming or reinforcing a mental habit.

    Did any of the suggested Meditation techniques stand out?

    In the case of a distraction, when I feel my mind begin to wonder, I need to first pay attention to that distraction by acknowledging the distraction. Experience it without judging or reacting and then let it go and slowly come back to a sense of mindfulness.
