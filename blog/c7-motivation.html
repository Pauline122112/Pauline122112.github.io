The Art Of Self Motivation

    How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

    There are three types of happiness which can be described as pleasure, passion and purpose. Acknowleging these three distinction will lead to what we see as a meaningful higher purpose in our lives. When this develops, our work can become a source of sustainable happiness for us.

    Discovering values and higher purpose

    What are your core values?
    My core values are to ensure I manage everything with integrity. To always ask questions if I'm unsure. To help and support people. To acknowledge my own faults.
    What do you stand for?
    In regards to my background, being indigenous, I stand for the rights of my Indigenous whanau, I stand to make a difference. To give my people an opportunity to express themselves through the creative arts and web development. I stand to change the way many in this country view my people as a stereotype. I stand to make a difference, and to see change.

    How did you find the resilience ideas and meditation exercise?

    I found the calming to be frustrating to begin with. Having to think of a situation where I failed made me feel uncomfortable. Usually, I am able to compartmentalise situations that have impacted me in a negative way. The thought that I had to provoke a failed situation was difficult to hold on to for a long time because I was not use to acknowledging the situation. When it came to acknowledging success. I found to relax a lot more. Overall, I found it relaxing, but I do feel it is something that I will continuously need to practice.

    Any other muses?

    The idea of learning Optimism and unlearning Pessimism really struck a cord with me. I found that sometimes I exhibited Pessimistic traits but I feel we are people who think this way collectively, and yes, Optimism can be taught and I think thats what is difficult for people like myself to grasp, that something so simple can be learn't, yet, I have not taken the time to learn it, until now.
